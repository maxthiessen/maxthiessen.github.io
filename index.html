Maximilian Thiessen

Hey there, my name is Max. I am a PhD student supervised by Prof. Thomas Gärtner in the machine learning research unit at TU Wien, Austria. Before that, I studied computer science at the University of Bonn under the supervision of Dr. Tamás Horváth. My main research areas are learning with graphs, active learning, and learning theory. I try to bridge (graph) convexity theory and learning theory. More recently I have started working on graph representations going beyond the Weisfeiler-Leman isomorphism test.
